Creamy Chicken & Broccoli Rice

A simple, delicious, and complete meal using your ingredients.

Ingredients

  • check_circle 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • check_circle 1 cup of white rice
  • check_circle 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • check_circle 1 cup heavy cream
  • check_circle 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • check_circle 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • check_circle Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Cook the rice according to package directions. While it cooks, season the diced chicken with salt and pepper.

  2. 2

    In a large skillet, cook the chicken over medium-high heat until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

  3. 3

    In the same skillet, saut√© the minced garlic for about 30 seconds. Add the broccoli florets and cook until tender-crisp.

  4. 4

    Reduce heat to low, pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce has thickened slightly.

  5. 5

    Return the chicken to the skillet. Add the cooked rice and stir everything together until well combined and heated through. Season with additional salt and pepper if needed. Serve immediately.
